## Competition Summary

This competition represents one of my public machine learning projects on the DrivenData platform.

The objective was to gain practical experience in automatic speech recognition (ASR), sequence prediction, model evaluation, and iterative experimentation using speech data.

Model development followed an incremental versioning workflow, resulting in 22 public submissions and a final leaderboard rank of 90 among 394 ranked participants.
