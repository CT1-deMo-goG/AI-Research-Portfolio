This competition represents one of my early public machine learning projects on the DrivenData platform.

The objective was to gain practical experience in regression modeling, feature engineering, environmental data analysis, and iterative model development through reproducible computational experimentation.

Model development followed an incremental versioning workflow, resulting in 46 public submissions and continuous leaderboard improvements throughout the competition.
