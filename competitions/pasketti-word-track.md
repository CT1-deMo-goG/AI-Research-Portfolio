## Competition Summary

This competition is part of my public machine learning portfolio on the DrivenData platform.

The objective was to develop automatic speech recognition (ASR) models capable of producing word-level transcriptions of children's speech. The project involved iterative experimentation, model refinement, and evaluation using the public leaderboard.

The competition resulted in 18 completed public submissions and a final leaderboard rank of 140 among 655 ranked participants.
